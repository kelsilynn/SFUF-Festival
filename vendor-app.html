Vendor Application

UPRISING FEST VENDOR 2019 APPLICATION

Overview

Spiritual Family (+Universal Friends) is a non-profit organization geared towards providing safe space for individuals to express their talents and passions freely, while being supported by a loving community. As a vendor of SFUF, we are allowing you to make a profit in which elsewhere there would be none. The following is an application and agreement to the rights and reserves of vending at Uprising Fest 2019.

Instructions

Please read all of the information over, if you find yourself in agreement to our statements please attach any and all information, pictures, videos or links that relate to your products or services.

Disclaimer

We are not able to promise any return in funds or time put in to this festival, in the event that you do not sell what you are expecting to. We are not responsible for your belongings, and encourage that you have the proper protection for all valuables. This includes but is not limited to: a full coherent watch over items and display area, lock boxes for cash, locked vehicles or tents for storage.

1. Consideration / Compensation:

(a) Uprising Fest will be allotted space for that of 10 vendors and it is not first come, first serve. Each application will be considered by that of an 8 person panel team. Applications will be open March 25th through April 25th, releasing the final outcome April 30th 2019. We’d like to thank all applicants for their time and creativity and wish the best for any involvement.

(b) In exchange for the full service fee (see below) each vendor will receive the space necessary to display your products and or services for the duration of the main event, Saturday June 8th 10am to 10pm. This may include a table, chairs, electrical outlets, and a backdrop if requested.

(c) Along with the space provided, you will be listed among all graphics and event information across a multitude of online sites. Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat and our very own webpage coming March 2019. You will be given the option to self promote under any and all outlets you currently use. You may also promote publicly at the event, this includes posters amongst the forest, fliers among cars, and word of mouth.

2. Service Fee:

As a non-profit organization, we are only able to provide this experience through ticket sales. Any and all money collected, goes directly into the production of this event. We have determined that in exchange for your ability to make a profit, a fee of $15 will be charged to put towards creating this event and in turn your space there. This is half that of an attendee ticket, and you will not be charged parking. Once we make our decision on the 10 total vendors, we will accept payments.

3. Vendor Agreement:

Once the application is received, and all 10 vendors have been decided upon, it is up to each vendor to arrive to the event prepared. This means requesting all necessary equipment between April 1st and June 1st 2019. Your name will be guest listed as VENDOR, when you arrive to the venue please have your ID ready. You will then be directed to parking, and upon request be given a tour of the venue. We are welcoming to our vendors to camp Friday June 7th and Saturday June 8th 2019, however some may choose to arrive day of.

(a) If chosen to camp for the duration of the weekend, you will be encouraged to set up your display area Friday before bed. This allows you proper time Saturday morning to wake up and have breakfast before getting to work.

(b) If chosen to arrive day of, Saturday June 8th you will have less time to prepare your display area and in turn make a profit. We will not allow any vendor to arrive later than 7pm on the day of. This is due to reducing high volumes of traffic near the end of the event.

There will be no refund of the service fee in the event that you do not arrive by 7pm Saturday June 8th 2019, or in the event you do not arrive at all.

4. Vendor Application:

Please provide attachments of any information, pictures, videos or links that relate to your products or services in order to properly consider each vendor for an official vendor spot for Uprising Fest 2019. All applications need to be sent to spiritualfamilyuprisingfest@gmail.com

5. Vendor Appreciation:

We would like to thank each individual for their time and dedication to their crafts. It is of our highest honors to have even been considered as a host to that of your physical, emotional, and spiritual creative outlets. We hope that any decision here after, will not discourage you from your passions and only fuel you to create more.
