Our Story

Spiritual Family (+Universal Friends) is an experience based project dedicated to providing safe space for individuals to express their passions and talents freely.

Today we bring you a chance at new beginnings, a place for all to find a home in this world! We are a community of like minded people coming together as one. SFUF is geared towards learning and growing providing equal opportunities to all. We have, and always will be a grassroots campaign ~ planting seeds of hope for years to come. We invite you to join us in celebrating life, love, creativity and expression.
